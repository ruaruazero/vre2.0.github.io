关于数据库


            该数据库包括了禽流感和非人类哺乳动物流感监测数据、与病毒提取物相关的人类临床数据、从提取物中分离的病毒的表型特征、流感病毒公共知识库中现有的所有基因组和蛋白质组数据、该资源还把宿主监测和临床数据与所有具有良好特征的流感病毒毒株的序列和表型数据联系起来，综合了流感病毒的各种数据,提供了持续改进的分析可视化工具,用于流感病毒数据挖掘和假设生成,还包含很多AMR在基因组和基因水平上的数据，通过机器学习构建可以预测所提交的基因组中和AMR表型和其遗传决定因素相关的信息。适用于对微生物耐药性（antimicrobial resistance，AMR）的研究，可对用户提供的序列及其它相关数据进行分析和可视化,具有预测新变异蛋白，注释相关表型序列标记及表型效应。
            允许用户使用进化树查看器对基于序列关联元数据的进化树节点进行着色。从使用标准化方案的全基因组和蛋白质组策展开始，“靶向”方法满足研究人员的特定需求，以及利用高通量实验数据（例如微阵列，蛋白质组学）和文献的综合策略。该网站支持浏览，查询，数据可视化以及以标准格式下载原始和精选数据的能力。提供了用于数据存储,分享的个人工作空间,促进了流感诊断,治疗及疫苗研制与研究，为病原体提供全面的生物信息学资源，包括持续注释的基因组，蛋白质组和代谢途径数据，以促进对策的研究，包括药物，疫苗和诊断方法。

			病毒性疾病爆发往往每几年在人群中发生一次(每1-4年)。在此类爆发期间，确定病原体和比较基因组分析对于限制病毒传播和确定合适的治疗方案至关重要。虽然防止此类疫情的第一道防线是临床报告和自动监测，但病毒分离物的“湿实验室”实验也很重要。生物信息学工具和数据库资源提供有关已知病毒的基因组结构和表型特征的信息，可以通过支持数据挖掘来开发值得对新兴菌株进行深入实验室实验的假设，从而提高这一过程的效率。
            VRE(Virus Research Encyclopaedia database)建立于2022年5月2日星期一，由有你好果汁吃团队开发和维护。该团队由干子凌、刘博、王新来和任博文组成。干子凌负责网站的开发和维护，刘博负责网站的美化和宣传，王新来负责提供前沿的信息和算法导向，任博文负责和其他网站建立合作关系。其功能之全、操作之便利、更新速度之快以及涵盖数据量之广在业内首屈一指，一经上线，广受广大科研工作者的好评。

            在未来，我们将会开发出更具有针对性的工具、拓宽数据库的类型以及研发出更精确的算法。我们希望这是一个长久年轻和保持活力的科研平台，他永远没有局限。


			




			
About database


            The database includes avian and non-human mammalian influenza surveillance data, human clinical data associated with viral extracts, phenotypic characteristics of viruses isolated from extracts, all genomic and proteomic data available in the public knowledge base of influenza viruses, and the resource also links host surveillance and clinical data to sequence and phenotype data of all well-characterized influenza virus strains, synthesizing data from influenza viruses and providing continuously improved analytical visualization tools, It is used for influenza virus data mining and hypothesis generation, and also contains a lot of DATA on AMR at the genomic and genetic level, and through machine learning, it is possible to predict the information in the submitted genome related to the AMR phenotype and its genetic determinants. Suitable for the study of antimicrobial resistance (AMR), it analyzes and visualizes user-supplied sequences and other related data, predicts new variant proteins, annotates relevant phenotypic sequence markers and phenotypic effects.
            Allows users to color evolution tree nodes based on sequence association metadata using the Evolution Tree Viewer. Starting with genome-wide and proteome curation using standardized protocols, the "targeted" approach addresses the specific needs of researchers, as well as comprehensive strategies utilizing high-throughput experimental data (e.g., microarrays, proteomics) and literature. The site supports browsing, querying, data visualization, and the ability to download raw and selected data in a standard format. Provides a personal workspace for data storage, sharing, facilitates influenza diagnosis, treatment, and vaccine development and research, and provides a comprehensive bioinformatics resource for pathogens, including continuously annotated genomic, proteomic, and metabolic pathway data to facilitate research into countermeasures, including drugs, vaccines, and diagnostic methods.


			




			
About our team



			Outbreaks of viral diseases tend to occur in the population every few years (1-4). During such outbreaks, identification of pathogens and comparative genomic analysis are essential to limit viral transmission and determine appropriate treatment options. While the first line of defense against such outbreaks is clinical reporting and automated surveillance, "wet lab" experiments with virus isolates are also important. Bioinformatics tools and database resources provide information about the genomic structure and phenotypic characteristics of known viruses, and can improve the efficiency of this process by supporting data mining to develop hypotheses that merit in-depth laboratory experiments on emerging strains.
            VRE (Virus Research Encyclopaedia database) was established on Monday, May 2, 2022 and is developed and maintained by the YouHao Juice Eat team. The team consists of Gan Ziling, Liu Bo, Wang Xinlai and Ren Bowen. Gan Ziling is responsible for the development and maintenance of the website, Liu Bo is responsible for the beautification and publicity of the website, Wang Xinlai is responsible for providing cutting-edge information and algorithm orientation, and Ren Bowen is responsible for establishing cooperative relations with other websites. Its complete function, convenient operation, fast update speed and wide range of data coverage are second to none in the industry, and once launched, they are widely praised by the majority of scientific researchers.
            We hope to provide a stable and efficient scientific research and experimental platform for the majority of scientific researchers, and contribute to the long and arduous tug-of-war to overcome the disease. We also welcome users to put forward opinions and suggestions, and we will build a more efficient scientific research platform together.
            In the future, we will develop more targeted tools, broaden the types of databases, and develop more precise algorithms. We want this to be a long-term young and dynamic research platform, which never has limitations.
